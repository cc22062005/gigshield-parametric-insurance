# GigShield AI: Concept Video Script & Storyboard
**Target Length**: 1-minute (For Hackathon Judges)
**Tone**: Fast-paced, empathetic, high-tech, resolved.

---

### [0:00 - 0:10] The Problem
**Visual**: Split screen. Left side shows dark, heavy rain pounding a city street. Right side shows a delivery rider (Raj) staring at his phone looking stressed. The Zomato/Swiggy app says "High Demand" but he can't ride.  
**Audio (Voiceover - Urgent)**: *"For millions of delivery partners in India, heavy rain doesn't mean a day off. It means losing wages they rely on for survival."*  
**Text Overlay**: The Gig Economy Reality. No Work = No Pay.

### [0:10 - 0:25] The Solution (GigShield Intro)
**Visual**: Switch to the GigShield mobile UI "Landing Page". Raj signs up and views his AI Risk Score badge. He buys the "₹69 Standard Plan". Fast animation showing coins dropping.  
**Audio (VO - Empathetic & Tech-focused)**: *"Enter GigShield AI. A parametric insurance platform built for the gig economy. Riders buy bite-sized weekly policies. No lengthy forms. No adjusters."*  
**Text Overlay**: AI-Risk Profiling. Weekly Micro-Policies.

### [0:25 - 0:40] The Trigger (The Oracle)
**Visual**: Switch to "Admin Oracle Demo" screen. Mouse clicks on "Simulate Rain Storm". Map shows a red storm blob over "Zone 4". API log streams across the terminal: *API MATCH: Threshold exceeded for Continuous Rain.*  
**Audio (VO - Fast)**: *"When severe weather, pollution spikes, or curfews hit, our system pulls data from automated oracles like OpenWeatherMap and NewsAPI. If the disruption threshold is crossed... boom."*

### [0:40 - 0:50] The Payout & Fraud Defense
**Visual**: Cut back to the Rider Dashboard. A neon green notification pops up: "SUCCESS: ₹500 Deposited to Wallet".  
Secondary visual flashes briefly: "FRAUD DETECTED: GPS SPOOF BLOCKED", showing the robustness of the platform.  
**Audio (VO - Triumphant)**: *"Payouts are instant and automatic. Protected by state-of-the-art AI fraud-detection that blocks GPS spoofing in real-time."*

### [0:50 - 1:00] Conclusion / CTA
**Visual**: Montage of 3 different riders looking relieved, overlay text reads "Protected. Resilient. GigShield." End on a stark black screen with the GigShield Logo and "Try the Demo".  
**Audio (VO - Confident)**: *"GigShield AI. Ride fearless. We cover the losses."*
